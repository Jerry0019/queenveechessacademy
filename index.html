Empowering Displaced Children, One Move at a Time

Transforming lives through the power of chess.

Support Us Learn More

At Chess in IDP camp, we are dedicated to transforming lives through the power of chess. Founded with the belief that every child deserves a chance to thrive, we empower internally displaced children in Northern Nigeria with strategic thinking, emotional resilience, and a renewed sense of hope.

Through our flagship program, the Chess in IDP Camps Initiative, we use chess as a tool to address trauma, enhance critical thinking, and foster belonging among children affected by displacement. Our work is grounded in the idea that chess can be more than a game—it can be a pathway to healing and growth.

We have helped 50 children return to school, providing them with the opportunity to continue their education and build a brighter future.

We supported four boys from IDP camps to represent Adamawa State at the National Youth Games, with one of them achieving an impressive 4th place on Board 4.
